Incline Press, barbell, pronated grip, standard width

Set up:

  • Start position: lying on bench with arms extended, holding the bar.
  • Breath in as you lower the weight.
  • Exhale and squeeze the grips as you raise the weight.

Coaching Cues

  • Keep shoulder blades retracted throughout the movement. Don’t round the shoulder at the top of the movement.
  • Keep head neutral (don’t tuck your chin), brace your abs and squeeze your glutes as you raise the weight.
  • Try to keep your neck and face relaxed.

Pro Tip

  • Focus your total mind on your pecs doing the work.
  • Imagine your pecs getting stronger with each rep.
  • Count down your reps!
